Number Guess!

Directions/Game Instructions:

Objective: Guess the correct number chosen by the computer. Directions: Enter the number you believe to be chosen by the computer in the input field below and click the 'Submit' button.

If you guess the correct number, you win the game and the computer will congradulate you on a job well done.

If your number guess is incorrect (meaning too low or too high), then the computer will notify you with an idication of whether to make your guess a higher or lower number.

You will have 3 chances to guess the correct answer before you lose the game.

If you wish to start over before the game ends, you may click the 'Reset' button at the bottom of the page.
